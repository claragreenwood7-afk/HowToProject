Mini Muffin Tin Doughnut Holes

From "The Complete Baking Book for Young Chefs"

Info

  • Makes 24 Doughnut Holes
  • Total Time: 50 Minutes Plus 10 Minutes Cooling Time

Prepare Ingredients

Doughnuts

  • Vegetable oil spray
  • 1 1/3 c. all-purpose flour
  • 2 T. cornstarch
  • 1 1/2 t. baking powder
  • 1/2 t. salt
  • 1/4 t. ground nutmeg
  • 1/2 c. sugar
  • 1/2 c. buttermilk
  • 4 T. butter, melted
  • 1 large egg plus 1 large yolk

Topping

  • 4 T. butter
  • 1 t. ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 c. sugar

Gather Baking Equipment

  • 24-cup mini muffin tin
  • 4 bowls (1 large, 2 medium, 1 small microwave-safe)
  • Whisk
  • Rubber spatula
  • 1-T. Measuring spoon
  • Toothpick
  • Oven mitts
  • Cooling rack
  • Pastry brush

Start Baking

For the doughnuts:

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees. Spray mini muffin tin well with vegetable oil spray
  2. In medium bowl, whisk together flour, cornstarch, baking powder, salt nutmeg and 1/2 cup sugar.
  3. In large bowl, whisk buttermilk, 4 T. melted butter, egg, and egg yolk until well combined
  4. Add flour mixture and use rubber spatula to stir until just combined and no dry flour is visible
  5. Spray 1-T. measuring spoon with vegetable oil spray. Use greased measuring spoon to scoop 1 T. batter into each muffin tin cup.
  6. Place muffin tin in oven. Bake until toothpick inserted in center of 1 doughnut hole comes out clean, about 10 minutes.
  7. Use oven mitts to remove muffin tin from oven (ask an adult for help). Place muffin tin on cooling rack and let doughnut holes cool in muffen tin for 10 minutes.

For the topping:

  1. In small microwave-safe bowl, melt remaining 4 T. butter. In second medium bowl, use clean rubber spatula to stir together cinnamon and remaining 1/2 c. sugar.
  2. Carefully remove doughnut holes from muffin tin and transfer directly to cooling rack (ask an adult for help-muffin tin will be hot). Place cooling rack in rimmed baking sheet. Use pastry brush to paint doughnut holes all over with melted butter (use all of butter). Then, roll each doughnut hole in cinnimon sugar to coat all over. Serve and enjoy.
